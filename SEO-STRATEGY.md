## Key Differentiator Strategy

Natamy has ONE thing no competitor has: **the AI + automation angle**. Every competitor sells Google Ads + social media. Natamy sells the full funnel: ads + AI chatbot qualification + automated follow-up.

**Position as:** "A unica especialista em Portugal que combina trafego pago com automacao de IA para stands automoveis."

Content should hammer this angle relentlessly:
- "Porquê pagar por leads se ninguem os segue?" (Why pay for leads if nobody follows up?)
- "O seu vendedor nao e um call center" (Your salesperson isn't a call center)
- "A IA nao tira ferias" (AI doesn't take holidays)

This is the competitive moat. Own it.
